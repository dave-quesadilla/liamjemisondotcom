As an undergraduate at Portland State University I wrote an [honors thesis](https://pdxscholar.library.pdx.edu/honorstheses/910/) about DC (difference- or perhaps delta-) convex functions under Dr. Mau Nam Nguyen. DC functions are a super set of convex functions -- any function expressible as the difference of two convex functions is a DC function -- and are of interest as the subject of efficient optimization algorithms.
